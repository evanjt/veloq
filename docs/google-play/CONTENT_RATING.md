## Rating Justification

Veloq is a fitness tracking application that:

- Displays only fitness and health data owned by the user
- Contains no user-generated content visible to others
- Has no social features or user interaction
- Contains no violent, sexual, or mature content
- Has no gambling or simulated gambling
- Does not reference controlled substances
- Has no in-app purchases or real-money transactions
- Does not target or appeal to children specifically

The app is suitable for all ages, though it is designed for and marketed to adult athletes who use fitness tracking services.
