## Merchant Account

To sell paid apps on Google Play, you need a Google Payments merchant account.

### Setup Steps

1. Go to Google Play Console → Setup → Payments profile
2. Click "Create payments profile"
3. Complete the merchant registration:
   - Business information
   - Tax information
   - Bank account for payouts

### Requirements

- Valid government ID
- Business registration (if applicable)
- Bank account in supported country
- Tax information (W-9 for US, VAT ID for EU, etc.)

### Payout Schedule

- Google takes a 15% commission (first $1M/year) or 30% thereafter
- Payouts are monthly once you reach the minimum threshold ($100 USD)
- Payout timeline is typically end of month following the earning month

## Pricing Strategy Notes

### Why $4.99?

- **No ongoing costs:** One-time purchase, no subscription
- **Quality positioning:** Signals a polished, maintained app
- **Sustainable development:** Supports continued updates
- **Ad-free guarantee:** Users pay once, never see ads

### Considerations

- F-Droid version will be free (open source requirement)
- Consider promotional pricing for launch
- Monitor competitor pricing in Health & Fitness category
