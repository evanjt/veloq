View your data

Access your Intervals.icu data on mobile with offline support.

🌎

Maps

View your activities on interactive maps with 3D terrain, route heatmaps, and timeline filtering. Scrub through charts and watch your position update in real-time.

📈

Charts

View heart rate, power, pace, elevation, and cadence charts from your Intervals.icu data.

🛠

Route tracking

Automatically detect repeated routes and sections. Compare efforts over time to see your progress on favourite climbs and stretches.

💪

Fitness

View your CTL/ATL/TSB fitness data and form zones as calculated by Intervals.icu.

📷

On-Device

Route and section detection runs entirely on your device. Your data never leaves your phone.

🔒

Privacy

All data stays on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no servers. Open source.
