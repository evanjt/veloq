Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 January 2026

Veloq operates primarily on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. The app fetches your fitness data directly from Intervals.icu and stores it locally for offline access.

Authentication Methods

Veloq supports two ways to connect to your Intervals.icu account:

API Key Mode (Local Only)

  • Your API key is stored encrypted on your device using platform secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android KeyStore)
  • The key never leaves your device — all API calls go directly to Intervals.icu
  • No backend servers involved

OAuth Mode (Connected)

  • Uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • A Cloudflare Worker proxy handles the token exchange to keep the client secret secure
  • The proxy temporarily stores a state parameter (5-minute TTL) for CSRF protection
  • Your access token is returned directly to your device and stored in secure storage
  • The proxy does not store your access token

Data We Fetch

From Intervals.icu (with your permission):

  • Activities (routes, metrics, streams)
  • Wellness data (HRV, sleep, weight)
  • Calendar and planned workouts
  • Sport settings (zones, thresholds)

Local Storage

All data is stored on your device only:

  • Credentials — Encrypted (SecureStore), retained until you disconnect
  • Activity data — 7 days cache, GPS tracks stored indefinitely
  • Preferences — Theme, map settings, retained until you clear app data

Third-Party Services

Intervals.icu

Your fitness data source. See their Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Map Tile Providers

Standard web requests, no personal data sent:

  • OpenFreeMap / OpenStreetMap (light mode)
  • CARTO (dark mode)
  • EOX Sentinel-2 (satellite imagery)
  • AWS Terrain Tiles (3D elevation)

Cloudflare Workers (OAuth mode only)

  • Temporarily stores OAuth state parameters (5-minute TTL)
  • May store webhook event notifications (24-hour TTL) for future push notification support
  • Does not store access tokens or personal data

No analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

None. The developer has no access to your data. All data flows directly between your device and Intervals.icu, or through standard map tile requests (no personal data).

Security

  • Credentials encrypted using platform secure storage
  • HTTPS/TLS for all network requests
  • OAuth state validation (CSRF protection)
  • Webhook signature verification (HMAC-SHA256)
  • No background telemetry or data collection

Disclaimer

Veloq is an independent, open-source project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Intervals.icu in any way. For information about how Intervals.icu handles your data, please refer to their privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Not intended for users under 13. No data is collected from any user.

Your Rights

  • View your data: All data is visible within the app
  • Delete your data: Uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings
  • Revoke access: Disconnect your account in app settings, or revoke the OAuth app in Intervals.icu settings

Changes

This policy may be updated. Check the "Last updated" date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
