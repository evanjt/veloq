Veloq brings your Intervals.icu training data to mobile with a focus on maps, visual analytics, and offline access.

<b>Map-First Visualisation</b>
View your activities on an interactive regional map with timeline filtering and route heatmaps. Switch between light, dark, and satellite map styles. Explore 3D terrain views with synchronised chart scrubbing.

<b>Rich Activity Analysis</b>
Dive into multi-metric charts for heart rate, power, pace, elevation, and cadence. Scrub through your activity with real-time map highlighting. View zone distributions, training metrics (TSS, intensity factor), and lap breakdowns.

<b>Route Intelligence</b>
Automatically detect repeated routes and track your performance over time. Compare efforts on the same course to see progress. Identify frequently-travelled sections with detailed match analysis.

<b>Fitness Tracking</b>
Monitor your CTL/ATL/TSB fitness model with form zone analysis. Track HRV, resting heart rate, sleep, and wellness metrics. View power curves, pace curves, and zone distributions by sport.

<b>Offline-First</b>
Aggressive caching ensures your data is always available. Sync your activities once and access them anywhere without an internet connection.

<b>Privacy Focused</b>
All data stays on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection. Your credentials are stored securely using platform encryption.

<b>Multi-Sport Support</b>
Full support for cycling, running, swimming, and 20+ activity types with sport-specific metrics and analysis.

<b>Requirements</b>
An Intervals.icu account with API key access. Get your API key from Intervals.icu Settings > Developer Settings.

<b>Open Source</b>
