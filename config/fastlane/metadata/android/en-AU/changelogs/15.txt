v0.2.0 (Feb 2026)

• Activity cards now show +/− change indicators for each metric
• Cards without map data are minimised automatically to save space
• Fitness chart combines CTL/ATL with form shown as a horizontal line, with zone boundary markers
• Activity feed: filter buttons unnested, search bar moves below the fold on load (swipe down to reveal), longer sparklines, and bottom-row chips are now tappable
• Maps: smaller start/end markers, trace line has a contrasting border for better visibility on satellite view, map expands into the card with overlaid text
• Charts: swap between time and distance on the x-axis, average value shown on line, improved scrubbing on fitness plot
• Power zones and intervals table added to activity detail
• Season best values now shown alongside personal records

Full details: https://github.com/evanjt/veloq/releases/tag/v0.2.0
